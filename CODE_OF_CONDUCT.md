# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers of HeroOS pledge to make participation in our community a respectful, harassment-free, and inclusive experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity or expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We are committed to fostering an open, welcoming, and professional environment for all contributors.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language  
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences  
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully  
- Focusing on what is best for the community and project  
- Showing empathy toward other community members  
- Helping others learn and improve  

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful conduct  
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments  
- Personal or political attacks  
- Public or private harassment  
- Publishing others’ private information without permission  
- Any conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting  

---

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior.

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.

Maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate in response to unacceptable behavior, including temporary or permanent bans from the project.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including but not limited to:

- GitHub Issues  
- Pull Requests  
- Discussions  
- Official Community Channels  
- Social Media Interactions Related to HeroOS  

It also applies when an individual is officially representing the project in public spaces.

---

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to the project maintainers.

Provide:

- Description of the incident  
- Relevant screenshots/logs (if applicable)  
- Links to related discussions/issues  

All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled confidentially where possible.

---

## Enforcement Guidelines

Maintainers may apply the following consequences depending on severity:

### Warning
For minor violations, a warning may be issued.

### Temporary Ban
For repeated or serious misconduct.

### Permanent Ban
For severe harassment, abuse, or repeated violations.
